how to buy

1

Set Up a Crypto Wallet

To buy $SHIRO, you'll need an Ethereum-compatible wallet like MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or Coinbase Wallet. Download the wallet app, create your account, and securely save your recovery phrase.

2

Fund Your Wallet with Ethereum (ETH)

You'll need ETH to buy $SHIRO and to cover gas fees. Purchase ETH on exchanges such as Binance or Coinbase, and transfer it to your wallet using your wallet address.

3

Buy $SHIRO on a Decentralized
Exchange (DEX)

With ETH in your wallet, connect to a DEX like Uniswap. Search for $SHIRO using its contract address to ensure accuracy. Enter the amount of ETH you want to swap, confirm the transaction, and $SHIRO will be sent to your wallet after paying the gas fees.

4

Add $SHIRO to Your Wallet

To see your $SHIRO tokens in your wallet, simply add the $SHIRO contract address to your wallet. Once added, your $SHIRO tokens will appear. Welcome aboard!
